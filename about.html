Our Story

Sonia's Fashion Boutique was founded by Sonia Uwase, a passionate fashion enthusiast with a dream to bring stylish, high-quality women's clothing to the Karongi Refugee Camp community. What started as a small home-based business has grown into a beloved fashion destination.

Our mission is to empower women in our community through fashion, helping them feel confident, beautiful, and authentic in every outfit they wear. We believe that everyone deserves to express their unique style without breaking the bank.

Today, we proudly serve hundreds of happy customers across Karongi and beyond, offering carefully curated collections that blend international trends with local preferences.
